## Learned
- Horizon preference wins for editor defaults: preferredEditor controls default editor behavior, and VSCodium may remain installed without stealing defaults unless preferredEditor is Codium. — source: previous-compaction-summary#Key-Decisions
- Negative-shaped Spirit records are not automatically invalid; cleanup should be conservative and use clarify, supersede, retire, or rehome rather than bulk deletion. — source: previous-compaction-summary#Key-Decisions
- The Ethernet failure was not DHCP or NetworkManager; kernel carrier loss preceded NetworkManager activation failure, and EEE disablement is a mitigation rather than a proven root cause. — source: previous-compaction-summary#Key-Decisions
- Bound Spirit bulk jobs with systemd MemoryMax and chunked work; unbounded trim/import scripts can consume tens of GiB and make the desktop appear to be out of memory. — source: cmd:journalctl --user -u spirit-bigtrim.service -b#23.6G memory peak
- Report 707’s active referent coverage claim was close but stale by one: it said 614 active and 5 without referents, while the fresh pre-repair query showed 615 active and 6 without. — source: cmd:python3 /tmp/parse_spirit_records.py /tmp/spirit-active-fresh.nota plus /home/li/primary/reports/system-maintainer/707-parallel-lanes-deployed.md
- meta-spirit Import is the forced owner-side change path currently available on the meta socket; after the upsert fix it can overwrite existing record ids in place. — source: cmd:meta-spirit /tmp/spirit-referent-repair-import.nota#Imported 6
- Active Spirit records should not be referent-less; if a record is junk or intentionally unindexed, retire it to Zero rather than keeping it active with empty referents. — source: session experience: six active empty-referent records were repaired and ztC was zeroed
- On ouranos, deploying an OsOnly shape dropped broad firmware and Home Manager units; this is unsafe for live bare-metal desktop generations unless model-specific firmware covers every needed device or the deployment shape is not made boot default. — source: cmd:bootctl status plus /run/current-system activation and firmware closure inspection
- Hardware-dependent CriomOS fixes should be driven from explicit hardware predicates in node or cluster data, not from ad-hoc host-local workarounds. — source: Spirit record m9yz
- The current subagent runner may fail immediately with stale-run reconciliation; do not assume dispatched workers are progressing unless status shows live progress or result files. — source: subagent-status:0ba7a262-668b-45d3-a8fd-104a6484ed5c
