numbered sub-reports; this frame and the synthesis bracket them:

- `1-reports-audit.md` — the ten reports (`624`–`633`): what each established and
  whether it is still accurate now (supersessions flagged).
- `2-intent-audit.md` — the Spirit records captured (`wfdt`, `2zed`, `7c71`,
  `my86`, `wqdi`), the two guardian rejections and what they taught, the coherent
  intent picture.
- `3-code-branch-audit.md` — the verified branch state across 8 repos, the
  meta-signal split landed on spirit main, the two open regressions.
- `4-open-items-audit.md` — beads, pending operator work, open design items, and
  worktree hygiene.
- `5-synthesis-new-moon.md` — the comprehensive synthesis: what's new, what's
  true now, what's next, and the meta-lessons.

Written incrementally so context can restart mid-assembly.
