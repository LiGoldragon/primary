**Add near the top (the purpose, above the exemplar framing):**

> Spirit is the intent layer made executable — a universal intent-capture tool for
> every human, not bespoke to one psyche or workspace. It holds durable intent as typed
> records and keeps the live store mutually-consistent by construction. (Spirit record
> `zjho`.)

**Add as load-bearing constraints (the same italic-headed style as the existing ones):**

> *Intent is filed by `domain` and `referent`.* The `domain` is the universal grounded
> subject an intent is about — a specific subject (`Parsing`, `Deployment`,
> `Parenting`) drawn from an open, curated, growable vocabulary (`category`/`topic`
> retired), grouped under broad `area`s. The vocabulary is a variable-depth tree: depth
> tracks intent density, so sparse life-areas stay two-tier (`(Health Body)`) while the
> dense `Software` area runs three tiers (`(Software (Quality PropertyBasedTesting))`)
> over ~200 universal subjects. The `referent` is the concrete named instance an intent
> governs — `spirit`, `nota`, `theSouthField` — held in a per-person, guardian-gated
> registry of camelCase atoms, never a domain. Most software intent carries both: a
> universal domain plus a workspace referent. (Spirit records `ywua`, `oyli`, `0zi7`,
> `4wt3`, `4hb1`.)

> *The guardian is Spirit's one semantic admission authority.* A single LLM judgment —
> daemon as gate, agent as brain — gates every change that touches the live arrow set,
> returning a binary typed verdict, fail-closed. The model checks everything semantic;
> only structural admission stays upstream (`zgi8`). It judges against the psyche's
> actual verbatim words and their context, not only the existing records (`tfpd`).
> Every change needs a justifying psyche statement; a deletion is justified by a
> statement such as a changed mind and needs no replacement (`r57r`). Registering a new
> referent is gated like a record (`ut6z`), and the guardian decides an entry together
> with the referents it introduces as one atomic accept-or-reject, so no orphan referent
> can pollute the store (`kasm`).
