# 558.6 — introspect — early (effort: large)

## introspect — migration assessment

**Role.** The prototype's inspection plane: a supervised daemon + thin CLI that asks live peer daemons over Signal for typed observations and projects one NOTA proof of a delivery. Never in the delivery path; never opens a peer's redb. Repos present: `introspect`, `signal-introspect`. **Missing: `meta-signal-introspect`** (confirms the seed gap).

**Overall: early / large.** introspect is honest, well-tested, and clean on the two edge axes (binary-config boundary, bare naming), and it already runs real Kameo actors over its own `introspect.sema`. But the spine is entirely hand-written: a blocking `listener.incoming()` daemon loop, a `signal_channel!` contract, no schema, no generated shell, no meta contract, and three of the same Kameo hazards 556 found on terminal.

### Migration path for introspect

introspect is the cleanest of the non-delivery components on its *edges* — binary-only daemon config and fully bare naming are already done, and it genuinely runs on Kameo over its own sema-engine store. The distance is the *spine*, and it is large. Nothing can move on the daemon-shell axis until two shared pieces land that do not exist today: schema-rust-next emission for an introspect shell, and triad-runtime's actor substrate (`ActorMultiListenerDaemon` is absent from triad-runtime/src — only `RequestGate` exists). In parallel and independently, the contract work can start: lower `signal-introspect` from a real `.schema` through schema-next to kill `signal_channel!`, and stand up `meta-signal-introspect` to carry the inspection-authorization/redaction policy that currently squats in the ordinary contract. The cheap near-term wins that need no shared substrate are the three kameo-discipline fixes (RestartPolicy, supervised-actor instead of the leaked thread, spawn_blocking the redb store) and refreshing the docs to stop reading as "done." Because introspect is not in the delivery path, the execution-nexus axis is effectively n-a — it never carried the old executor — so the real ordering is: kameo/storage hygiene now → contracts to schema-next + create the meta contract → emitted shell + actor substrate once both upstreams ship.
