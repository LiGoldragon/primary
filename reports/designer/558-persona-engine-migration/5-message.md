### Migration path for message

Almost nothing remains *inside* the daemon ‚Äî it is already the target shape (emitted shell on the actor substrate, generated nexus runner, schema-next planes, binary config, no signal-executor, no signal_channel in src). The remaining work is contract-level and largely shared with router. First, migrate the `signal-message` crate off `signal_channel!` to a schema-next-derived contract (add schema-rust-next + build.rs, lower `signal-message.concept.schema` into a generated `src/schema/`); once router also speaks that wire, the `RouterForwarder` schema‚Üîwire translation seam in `src/router.rs` collapses to a direct pass-through. Both are small relative to the daemon work other components still face.
