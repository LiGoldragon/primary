All three repos are present; the meta crate still carries `owner-signal-` residue.

## What is genuinely landed

Two things are real and worth protecting through the rewrite: the daemon is already on the actor-native task substrate (`triad-runtime/src/async_runtime.rs` `AsyncMultiListenerDaemon`, with the `RequestGate` kameo actor and a single `RepositoryLedgerStoreActor` owning the redb store — confirmed by the regression guard `tests/store.rs:500` `daemon_source_does_not_reintroduce_blocking_listener_shape`), and the NOTA/binary startup boundary is fully correct (one rkyv `DaemonConfiguration`, NOTA rejected, with tests). So the substrate is not a blocker here — the gaps are the emitter, the nexus runner, and the schema-next contracts.

## Migration path for repository-ledger

repository-ledger needs the deepest rework of the 555 set because nothing of the schema/nexus stack has touched it. The dependency order is: (1) author one shared `repository-ledger.schema` carrying both the ordinary and meta legs, add schema-next to both contract crates, emit `src/schema/`, and delete the two `signal_channel!` invocations plus the `.concept.schema` sketches; (2) regenerate the daemon shell from that schema via build.rs (GenerationDriver + NexusDaemonShape), emit `src/schema/daemon.rs`, and collapse the bin to `<Daemon>::run_to_exit_code()`; (3) replace the signal-executor `Executor`/`Lowering`/`CommandExecutor`/`ObserverSet` path with the generated NexusWork → NexusAction runner on `triad-runtime` `Runner::drive`, folding the existing `Store` command/query logic into nexus actions; (4) drop the hand-written `ToSemaOperation`/`ToSemaOutcome` impls and the signal-sema dependency, letting the emitter own SEMA routes, and tighten the store/spool actors (RestartPolicy, spawn_blocking for redb, retained ticker handle). Each step is gated on shared substrate (generated nexus runner, schema-rust-next daemon emission, schema-next contract derivation) landing first; until then, the honest status is signal_channel! contracts + signal-executor execution on a hand-written shell that happens to sit on the right actor substrate. Final cleanup: refresh ARCHITECTURE/INTENT to the schema-next + nexus target and scrub the `owner-signal-` residue from the meta crate.
