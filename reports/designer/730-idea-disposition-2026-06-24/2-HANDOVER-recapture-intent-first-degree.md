## Why this handover exists (the correction)

A prior pass tried to do context-maintenance by extracting "intent
candidates" out of the 129 agent-written reports and asking the psyche to
ratify them. That is backwards. Per `skills/intent-log.md`: the intent
log records **only psyche statements — not agent-written files**. Intent
is primordial and first-degree; reports are agent output. Distilled
"intent" mined from reports is second-degree and must never be laundered
into a Spirit record by ratification.

Psyche's words this session:

> "reports are generated by agents, and the source of intent should be
> first-degree, with an intent-alignment to establish certainty"

> "I want to clear out all the reports now, and have a very clean, lean,
> relevant small quantity at any time"

## The goal

3. **Clear all `reports/designer/` entries to zero.** Keep only a clean,

## Method — first-degree intent alignment

Per `skills/intent-alignment.md`, `skills/human-interaction.md`,
`skills/intent-log.md`:

- **One focused question per turn, in plain chat prose** — NOT the
  structured questionnaire UI. Each question carries: the decision, why
  it matters, a recommended answer, and one or two alternatives.
- Start from the **most basic principle** and narrow: what is this whole
  body of work *for*, before any feature-level question.
- Capture only first-degree psyche statements as intent. Before any
  `spirit` write: reload `intent-log.md` + `spirit-cli.md`, **query the
  domain first** (read existing records — see below), use affirmative
  framing, set certainty from the wording and importance honestly. Then
  manifest into the affected `INTENT.md`.
- Never infer intent. Ask when unclear.

## Interview spine (opening questions)

- **Q1 (most basic):** In your own words, what is this whole body of work
  *for* — what are you building and why? (A one-sentence purpose beats a
  feature list.)
- **Q2:** Of the 11 threads above, which are the live core you intend to
  keep building, which are just work to track, and which are
  done/exploratory/abandon? (Go thread by thread.)
- **Q3 (per live thread):** What do you intend it to *be* — the durable
  shape — and what do you explicitly *not* want?
- Then narrow only where the answer changes the first useful slice.
