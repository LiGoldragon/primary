# 532 — Meta-supreme deep audit — frame and method

## What the psyche asked

*"Do a deep audit of everything — an engine analysis, an intent
maintenance, a content maintenance — a full meta supreme deep
left-right-up-down-center, check everything, find bad patterns, find good
patterns, find the pattern of pattern, find the intent of intent, and make
a meta-psyche report with multiple subagents making a psyche report each."*

Five dimensions, each a subagent psyche report; the orchestrator writes
the meta-synthesis — the **pattern of patterns** and the **intent of
intent**.

## The five subagent reports

1. **`1-engine-analysis.md`** — the schema-derived / triad engine, deep:
   how it actually works (NOTA → schema-in-rust → lower; the structural
   macro node; the Signal/Nexus/SEMA runtime triad; lowering-as-tokens;
   the Asschema removal), its health, and what the engine *is* at its
   core. Grounded in code.
2. **`2-code-patterns.md`** — bad patterns AND good patterns across the
   stack (extending report 529), file:line, plus the recurring
   *code*-meta-pattern: what is true of all the smells, and all the
   strengths.
3. **`3-intent-analysis.md`** — the Spirit intent log: the landscape,
   duplicates / stale / consolidation candidates (proposed, with
   tombstones — not executed by the agent), and **the intent of intent**
   — the deepest unifying psyche intent behind the records.
4. **`4-content-maintenance.md`** — the reports + skills surface across
   lanes: topic arcs, stale/agglomerate candidates (proposed Refreshes
   with landing witnesses), hygiene.
5. **`5-discipline-meta.md`** — the workspace's *own* patterns and
   disciplines (methods-on-nouns, NOTA, the triad, the intent layer, the
   single-argument rule): the good meta-patterns that recur and work,
   where the workspace breaks its own rules, and the unifying
   meta-discipline.

The highest-numbered **`6-overview.md`** is the orchestrator's
**meta-psyche report**: the pattern of patterns, the intent of intent, the
supreme synthesis.

## Method + boundaries

- Each subagent writes a genuine **Psyche report** into this directory,
  grounded in real evidence (file:line, record ids), and returns a
  structured summary for the synthesis.
- **Maintenance is proposed, not executed by the subagents.** Intent
  removals are destructive and irreversible (`skills/intent-maintenance.md`
  — tombstone first); report deletions need landing witnesses
  (`skills/context-maintenance.md`). The subagents find and propose with
  tombstones/witnesses; the **orchestrator** executes the safe, clear
  actions (mistaken-duplicate dedups, agglomerations) after review and
  surfaces the judgment calls to the psyche.
- The directory IS the meta-report; it is garbage-collected as one unit.
