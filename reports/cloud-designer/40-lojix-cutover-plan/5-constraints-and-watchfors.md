### ✅ confirmed — (A-SECURITY, VIOLATED/absent) Subprocess timeout/kill on nix/ssh. NixCommand::run (schema_runtime.rs:1838-1855) does `.output().await` with NO tokio::time::timeout wrapper and NO .kill() path. A hung ssh/nix child runs unbounded.
