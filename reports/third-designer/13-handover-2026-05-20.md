## Side notes

note: my /246 §4 default filter shipped with role-based variants
(`All | OperationsOnly | EffectsOnly`) rather than /246 §4's literal
sketch of `OnlyOperations { kinds: Vec<<Channel>OperationKind> }`.
Per-kind filtering would require extending `<Channel>OperationKind`
(generated by `emit_request_kind`) with rkyv + NOTA derives so the
kind enum can ride the wire inside the filter's `kinds: Vec<...>`
field — a separate refactor of the kind-emission code. Contracts
needing per-kind filtering today use `filter <CustomType>;` and
write their own match impl. The deviation is documented in
`macros/src/emit.rs` `emit_default_filter` and in
`macros/README.md`.
